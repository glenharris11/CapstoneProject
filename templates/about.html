About: Glen Harris

Glen Harris grew up in a small Arizona town with a love for soccer and an aversion to homework. As he got older, he pursued his passion all the way to competing at the college level. However, with each passing year, his excitement for learning also grew and grew. Glen’s curiosity and hunger for knowledge led him to graduate from the University of Arizona with a degree in psychology. To start building a career, Glen turned to real estate and established himself as a top realtor in the Phoenix-metro area. Glen started working for State Farm in 2022 as a commercial insurance adjuster and decided to utilize their tuition benefits to advance his education. That love of learning had never ceded, and soon he found himself ready for a new challenge. In 2023, with the support of his amazing wife, Glen enrolled in Eastern University’s Master of Data Science program. He has been completing his degree during the week nights after work and on the weekends. Armed with his Masters, Glen is anxious to begin this new chapter of life.
